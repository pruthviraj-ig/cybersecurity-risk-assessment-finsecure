# Cybersecurity Risk Assessment – FinSecure Ltd

## Overview

This project presents a structured cybersecurity risk assessment conducted for a fictional UK-based financial technology company, **FinSecure Ltd**.

The objective of this assessment is to identify, analyse, and prioritise cybersecurity risks, evaluate existing controls, and recommend mitigation strategies aligned with industry standards.

---

##  Business Context

FinSecure Ltd is a fintech organisation that provides digital payment solutions and processes sensitive customer data, including financial information and personally identifiable information (PII).

Due to the nature of its operations, the organisation is exposed to various cybersecurity risks affecting confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA triad).

---

##  Objectives

* Identify key cybersecurity risks
* Assess likelihood and impact of risks
* Evaluate existing controls
* Identify control gaps
* Recommend risk mitigation strategies

---

##  Methodology

The risk assessment follows a structured approach:

* Asset Identification
* Risk Identification
* Risk Analysis (Likelihood × Impact)
* Control Evaluation
* Gap Analysis
* Risk Treatment & Residual Risk

Risk scoring:

* Low (1), Medium (2), High (3)
* Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

---

## Key Risks Identified

* Unauthorized access to sensitive financial data
* Phishing attacks targeting employees
* Cloud misconfiguration risks
* Insider threats
* System downtime
* Data loss due to backup failure

---

## Key Control Recommendations

* Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
* Security awareness training
* Centralised logging & monitoring (SIEM)
* Backup testing & recovery planning
* Cloud security configuration reviews

---

## Framework Alignment

This assessment aligns with:

* ISO 27001 (Information Security Management)
* NIST Cybersecurity Framework
* GDPR (Data Protection & Privacy)
